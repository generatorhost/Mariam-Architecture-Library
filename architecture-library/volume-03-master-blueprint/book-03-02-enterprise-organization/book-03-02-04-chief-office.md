# Book 03.02.04 - Chief Office

**Version:** v4 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V03-B03-02-004

## Purpose
Define the Chief Office as the executive coordination layer for cross-enterprise direction and accountability.

## Scope
Covers chief roles, executive mandates, operating reviews, delegation, and coordination with boards and departments.

## Responsibilities
- Coordinate enterprise priorities.
- Translate board direction into operating mandates.
- Own executive decision forums.
- Delegate accountable missions to departments.

## Inputs
- Board decisions.
- Enterprise KPIs.
- Department reports.
- Strategic objectives.
- Escalation requests.

## Outputs
- Executive mandates.
- Chief office decisions.
- Delegated missions.
- Operating review outcomes.

## Interfaces
- Board governance.
- Department missions.
- Decision flow.
- Enterprise KPIs.

## Events
- ChiefMandateIssued
- ExecutiveReviewCompleted
- MissionDelegated
- ChiefDecisionRecorded

## Storage
- Chief office mandate ledger.
- Executive decision records.
- Operating review archive.

## Security
- Chief office actions require executive authority.
- Delegated mandates retain origin and approval trace.
- Sensitive executive decisions are classified.

## Metrics
- Mandate completion rate.
- Executive decision cycle time.
- Escalation resolution time.

## Tests
- Delegation trace tests.
- Executive authority tests.
- Mandate lifecycle tests.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Chief office decisions are traceable to board or strategy sources.
- Delegated missions have owners.
- Operating reviews produce follow-up records.
