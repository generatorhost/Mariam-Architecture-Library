# Book 02-13 - Phase 11 Opportunity Intelligence

**Version:** v2 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V02-B013


## Purpose
Define how Mariam identifies, ranks, and explains opportunities for users and organizations.

## Scope
Covers opportunity signals, scoring, recommendations, market context, fit analysis, and action paths.

## Strategic Objective
Turn scattered signals into governed, explainable opportunity pipelines.

## Systems Included
- Opportunity signal model
- Scoring and fit engine
- Recommendation explanation
- Action workflow handoff
- Opportunity knowledge base

## Main Deliverables
- Opportunity intelligence specification
- Signal source catalog
- Scoring criteria
- Recommendation UI requirements
- Feedback loop

## Dependencies
- Knowledge system.
- DNA operating system.
- Marketplace and freelance strategies.

## Risks
- Bad scoring can mislead users.
- Signal sources may have licensing or reliability constraints.
- Recommendations can feel opaque without explanations.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Recommendations include rationale and source references.
- Users can accept, reject, or correct opportunity signals.
- Scoring criteria are testable.

## Implementation Notes
- Separate signal collection from decision support.
- Make opportunity ranking adjustable by user or organization context.
- Preserve privacy boundaries when using DNA.
