# Book 02-20 - Phase 18 Marketplace

**Version:** v2 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V02-B020


## Purpose
Define marketplace capabilities for matching people, services, opportunities, and trusted delivery evidence.

## Scope
Covers marketplace profiles, listings, matching, reputation, transactions, dispute signals, and marketplace governance.

## Strategic Objective
Create a trusted ecosystem layer that compounds platform knowledge and delivery evidence.

## Systems Included
- Marketplace profiles
- Listing and offer model
- Matching engine
- Reputation evidence
- Transaction support
- Marketplace governance

## Main Deliverables
- Marketplace strategy specification
- Profile and listing schemas
- Matching criteria
- Reputation policy
- Governance and dispute workflow

## Dependencies
- Freelance automation.
- Opportunity intelligence.
- Enterprise governance and knowledge system.

## Risks
- Marketplace trust can collapse if reputation is weak.
- Transactions introduce regulatory and support complexity.
- Matching may encode bias without evaluation.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Listings and profiles are structured and reviewable.
- Reputation uses evidence rather than opaque scores alone.
- Marketplace actions are governed and auditable.

## Implementation Notes
- Stage marketplace features gradually.
- Start with discovery and matching before complex transactions.
- Keep trust and dispute workflows visible.
