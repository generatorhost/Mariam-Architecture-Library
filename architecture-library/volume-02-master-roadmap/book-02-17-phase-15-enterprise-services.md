# Book 02-17 - Phase 15 Enterprise Services

**Version:** v2 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V02-B017


## Purpose
Define service offerings and internal service patterns that organizations can run through Mariam.

## Scope
Covers service catalogs, intake, SLAs, fulfillment workflows, service ownership, and performance reporting.

## Strategic Objective
Turn enterprise work into accountable services with clear intake and delivery outcomes.

## Systems Included
- Service catalog
- Intake workflows
- SLA and priority model
- Fulfillment tracking
- Service analytics

## Main Deliverables
- Enterprise services blueprint
- Service definition template
- Intake form standards
- SLA policy
- Service reporting views

## Dependencies
- Workflow system.
- Enterprise governance.
- Organization model.

## Risks
- Services may duplicate existing workflow concepts.
- SLA metrics may be misleading without context.
- Service catalogs can become stale.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Services have owners, intake rules, expected outcomes, and reporting.
- Fulfillment status is visible.
- Service definitions can be versioned.

## Implementation Notes
- Use services for repeatable enterprise offerings.
- Keep intake ergonomic.
- Connect service performance to workflow telemetry.
