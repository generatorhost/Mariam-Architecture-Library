# Book 00-02 - Vision

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V00-B002


## Purpose
State the future Mariam is designed to make possible for organizations and independent workers.

## Scope
Covers strategic aspiration, product horizon, and the intended transformation of AI-enabled work.

## Principles
- Mariam should make complex work governable, inspectable, and continuously improvable.
- AI agents must strengthen human capability instead of hiding decisions.
- The long-term product should connect work, knowledge, markets, and operational memory.

## Operating Rules
- Evaluate roadmap items by contribution to the vision.
- Prefer capabilities that compound across domains.
- Avoid short-term convenience that creates long-term opacity.

## Acceptance Criteria
- The vision can guide roadmap prioritization.
- The vision is broad enough for multiple domains but concrete enough to constrain design.
- Future expansion can be judged against this document.
