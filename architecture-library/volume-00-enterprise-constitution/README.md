# Volume 00 - Enterprise Constitution

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V00-README


## Purpose
Volume 00 establishes the constitutional foundation for Mariam AI Enterprise OS. It defines the durable identity, principles, operating expectations, governance model, quality posture, security posture, engineering behavior, lifecycle model, and long-term direction that all later volumes must respect.

## Scope
This volume applies to every business domain, AI capability, product module, integration, contributor workflow, and release decision in Mariam AI Enterprise OS.

## Principles
- Constitutional rules are stable enough to guide multiple product releases.
- Every downstream volume must remain compatible with this foundation.
- Governance, quality, security, and engineering discipline are treated as product foundations.

## Operating Rules
- Update this volume only through explicit versioned changes.
- Trace roadmap, blueprint, specification, and testing decisions back to constitutional documents.
- Keep constitutional language implementation-independent unless a binding constraint is intentional.
