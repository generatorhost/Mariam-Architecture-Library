# Book 00-03 - Mission

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V00-B003


## Purpose
Define the operational mission that turns the vision into repeatable execution.

## Scope
Covers what Mariam must reliably do for users, teams, contributors, and operators.

## Principles
- Convert goals into governed workflows.
- Preserve knowledge produced during work.
- Coordinate AI and human contributors with accountability.

## Operating Rules
- Mission-critical workflows require explicit ownership and traceability.
- Outputs must be inspectable before they become authoritative.
- The system must support real operating environments, not demo-only paths.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Mission statements map to product objectives in Volume 01.
- Core workflows can be tested against mission outcomes.
- Operational success can be measured without relying on vague adoption claims.
