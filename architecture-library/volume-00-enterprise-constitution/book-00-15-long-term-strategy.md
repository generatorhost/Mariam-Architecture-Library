# Book 00-15 - Long-Term Strategy

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V00-B015


## Purpose
Define the durable strategic posture for Mariam across multiple product eras.

## Scope
Covers platform expansion, ecosystem growth, knowledge compounding, enterprise adoption, and responsible AI evolution.

## Principles
- Build a platform that compounds through reusable knowledge and governed execution.
- Expand domains through architecture, not ad hoc duplication.
- Keep the system model-independent and organization-aware.

## Operating Rules
- Prioritize foundations that unlock multiple future capabilities.
- Avoid vendor, domain, or workflow lock-in unless explicitly justified.
- Review long-term strategy at major release gates.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Volume 01 strategic books align with this document.
- Roadmap planning can identify foundation work versus feature work.
- Future expansion remains coherent with the enterprise OS identity.
