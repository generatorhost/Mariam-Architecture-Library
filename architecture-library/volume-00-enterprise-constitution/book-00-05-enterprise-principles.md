# Book 00-05 - Enterprise Principles

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V00-B005


## Purpose
Define how Mariam behaves as enterprise software for teams, organizations, and managed operations.

## Scope
Covers roles, tenancy, auditability, policy, scalability, and operational durability.

## Principles
- Enterprise users need control, audit, reliability, and predictable change.
- Policy must be enforceable across modules.
- Tenant and role boundaries must be explicit.

## Operating Rules
- Design enterprise workflows around permissions, records, and review states.
- Do not add shared capabilities without ownership and audit semantics.
- Treat operational reporting as a primary requirement.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Enterprise objectives in Volume 01 map to these principles.
- Future blueprints include role and audit considerations.
- Release readiness includes operational governance checks.
