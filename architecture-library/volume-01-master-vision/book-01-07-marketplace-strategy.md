# Book 01-07 - Marketplace Strategy

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V01-B007


## Purpose
Define the strategic shape of future marketplace capabilities.

## Scope
Covers supply, demand, trust, matching, service packaging, reputation, and transaction support.

## Principles
- Marketplace value depends on trust, fit, and delivery evidence.
- Matching should consider capabilities, context, history, and governance.
- Marketplace growth should reinforce platform knowledge.

## Operating Rules
- Do not introduce marketplace behavior before identity, permissions, and quality controls are defined.
- Treat reputation as evidence-based and appealable.
- Keep transaction-sensitive flows auditable.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Marketplace roadmap can be staged responsibly.
- Trust and governance requirements are explicit.
- Marketplace strategy aligns with freelance and remote work ecosystems.
