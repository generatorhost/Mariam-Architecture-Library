# Volume 01 - Master Vision

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V01-README


## Purpose
Volume 01 translates the enterprise constitution into a master product and business vision. It explains what Mariam should become, which objectives matter, how AI strategy supports the platform, and how business, freelance, remote work, marketplace, knowledge, DNA, and expansion strategies fit together.

## Scope
This volume covers strategic direction only. It does not define implementation architecture, sprint plans, or detailed specifications.

## Principles
- Strategy must translate constitutional intent into product direction.
- Vision documents should be specific enough to guide roadmap choices.
- Strategic expansion must preserve Mariam's enterprise OS identity.

## Operating Rules
- Use this volume before committing roadmap themes.
- Keep strategy independent from temporary implementation choices.
- Revise strategy only when the constitutional baseline or market assumptions materially change.
