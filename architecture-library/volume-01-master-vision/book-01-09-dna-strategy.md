# Book 01-09 - DNA Strategy

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V01-B009


## Purpose
Define the strategic use of Mariam DNA for personalization and organizational memory.

## Scope
Covers memory layers, consent, preference modeling, behavior patterns, team norms, and adaptive workflows.

## Principles
- DNA should improve fit while preserving control.
- Memory must be scoped by user, team, domain, and organization.
- Inferred DNA must remain explainable and correctable.

## Operating Rules
- Introduce DNA capabilities through explicit consent and settings.
- Record provenance for important DNA changes.
- Do not let DNA override policy, security, or user intent.

## Acceptance Criteria
- DNA strategy traces to MAL-V00-B007.
- Future roadmap can separate personal and enterprise memory.
- Users can understand and correct strategic memory.
