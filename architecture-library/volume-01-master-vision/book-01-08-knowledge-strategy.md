# Book 01-08 - Knowledge Strategy

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V01-B008


## Purpose
Define how Mariam captures, organizes, retrieves, and improves knowledge.

## Scope
Covers knowledge objects, provenance, search, reuse, decay, and governance.

## Principles
- Knowledge must be structured enough for reuse and transparent enough for trust.
- Provenance matters as much as content.
- Knowledge should improve through usage and correction.

## Operating Rules
- Attach source, owner, freshness, and confidence metadata where needed.
- Avoid treating generated text as verified knowledge.
- Support correction, supersession, and archival states.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Knowledge strategy traces to DNA, AI, and quality principles.
- Future specifications can define knowledge object schemas.
- Generated knowledge is clearly distinguished from approved knowledge.
