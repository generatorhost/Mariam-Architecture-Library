# Book 01-02 - Enterprise Objectives

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V01-B002


## Purpose
Define measurable objectives for enterprise adoption and value creation.

## Scope
Covers organizational control, productivity, auditability, operating visibility, and repeatable delivery.

## Principles
- Objectives must be outcome-oriented.
- Enterprise adoption depends on trust and integration readiness.
- Operational value must survive beyond a demo.

## Operating Rules
- Frame objectives in terms of controlled workflows, reduced rework, and improved visibility.
- Connect objectives to future metrics where possible.
- Reject objectives that cannot be governed or verified.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Objectives can feed Volume 02 roadmap epics.
- Each objective maps to one or more constitutional principles.
- Objectives are clear enough for acceptance planning.
