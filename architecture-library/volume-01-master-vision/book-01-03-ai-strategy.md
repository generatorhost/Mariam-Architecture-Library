# Book 01-03 - AI Strategy

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V01-B003


## Purpose
Define how AI capabilities should advance the Mariam platform.

## Scope
Covers agent roles, model independence, evaluation, prompt governance, and AI-assisted execution.

## Principles
- AI strategy prioritizes useful orchestration over novelty.
- Agents should have bounded authority and measurable outputs.
- Model choice should remain replaceable.

## Operating Rules
- Separate AI recommendation, drafting, review, and execution responsibilities.
- Capture evaluation criteria for high-impact AI workflows.
- Design for provider portability and fallback.

## Acceptance Criteria
- AI roadmap items trace to MAL-V00-B006.
- AI features include evaluation and governance expectations.
- No AI strategy depends on undocumented autonomous authority.
