# Book 01-05 - Freelance Ecosystem

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V01-B005


## Purpose
Define Mariam's strategic role for freelancers and independent operators.

## Scope
Covers profile intelligence, proposal support, delivery workflows, client communication, portfolio memory, and trust.

## Principles
- Freelancers need leverage without losing authenticity.
- Work history should become reusable operating memory.
- Client-facing outputs must remain reviewable by the freelancer.

## Operating Rules
- Support freelancers with structured workflows, not uncontrolled automation.
- Keep ownership of identity and client communication clear.
- Preserve evidence of work quality and delivery commitments.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Freelance strategy can inform marketplace and DNA strategy.
- User trust and review paths are explicit.
- Future modules can support independent work without enterprise assumptions.
