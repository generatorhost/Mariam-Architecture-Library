**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft

## Purpose
Define expansion directions that preserve coherence as Mariam grows.

## Scope
Covers new domains, integrations, AI capabilities, ecosystem partnerships, and enterprise maturity.

## Principles
- Expansion should strengthen the enterprise OS foundation.
- New capabilities should reuse primitives where possible.
- Strategic optionality is protected by modular architecture and documentation discipline.

## Operating Rules
- Assess expansion ideas against identity, mission, AI, governance, and security principles.
- Record assumptions and dependencies before roadmap commitment.
- Prefer expansion that compounds knowledge, trust, and operating leverage.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Expansion themes can seed future roadmap volumes.
- Strategic risks are visible before implementation.
- Growth remains aligned with long-term strategy.
