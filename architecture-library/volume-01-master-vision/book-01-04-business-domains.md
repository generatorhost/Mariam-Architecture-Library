# Book 01-04 - Business Domains

**Version:** v1 draft
**Status:** Draft
**Document ID:** MAL-V01-B004


## Purpose
Identify the business domains Mariam may support through governed workflows.

## Scope
Covers operations, sales, marketing, finance support, delivery, support, knowledge work, and management workflows.

## Principles
- Domains should share platform primitives rather than isolated products.
- Domain expansion must respect enterprise governance.
- Domain workflows should generate reusable knowledge.

## Operating Rules
- Add domains through documented specifications.
- Define domain vocabulary before implementation.
- Prefer domain templates that can be audited and customized.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Initial domain list supports roadmap planning.
- Domain strategy traces to enterprise and quality principles.
- Future blueprints can define shared primitives.
